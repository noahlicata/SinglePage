My Portfolio

AutoMatik

AutoMatik is an online vehicle shopping application with a client side and an admin side. Users can browse through the selection of cars, use different filters to narrow down their searches, and save any favorites to their likes for later viewing. Admins can upload new vehicles to the database, and see a wide range of statistics about customers, sales, top selling brands, and much more. Made over the course of three weeks, this application uses React for the front end, and Ruby on Rails for the back end.

Denver City Limits

This project is an informational application for the upcoming (and in no way fabricated) Denver City Limits music festival. Users can browse through the lineup of artists, read their bios, and use the schedule page to see when each artist is playing, and on which stage. Users can also sign in to purchase tickets, which are available in two tiers, General Admission and VIP. Made over the course of just seven days, this project uses React for the front end, and Ruby on Rails for the back end.

Stacy's Designs

Stacy's Designs is an actively deployed informational application for a solo beauty practice. Users visit this website to book appointments and find general information about the business like location, hours, and different contact options. Users can also submit a contact form which forwards an email to the business owner with the form details.
